AI Class Project

Exploring how artificial intelligence is transforming economics, decision-making, and the limited-service restaurant industry.

This project explores the expanding role of artificial intelligence in the limited-service restaurant (LSR) industry, focusing on its effects on logistics, labor, forecasting, and operational efficiency. AI technologies are increasingly embedded in tools such as demand prediction models, automated scheduling systems, and intelligent supply-chain platforms. The project investigates how these applications reshape daily operations and long-term strategic planning, enabling firms to reduce waste, optimize labor, and improve delivery reliability. It also highlights how variability in AI adoption influences competition, with some firms leveraging advanced analytics to gain meaningful operational advantages. Overall, the project provides a detailed overview of the opportunities and challenges AI presents as it becomes central to LSR business operations.
